How to make the Juiciest Burgers

No more dry burgers, these are juicy and the spices are easy to add or take away

Indgredients

  • 2 pounds of ground beef.
  • 1 egg.
  • 3/4 cupd dre bread.
  • 3 tablespoons evaporated milk.
  • 2 tablespoons Worcestershire Sauce.
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper.
  • 2 gloves garlic, minced.
    •

    Steps

    1. Preheat grill to heat.
    2. grab a large mixing bowl.
    3. Mix ground beef, egg, dry bread crumbs, evaporated milk, Worcestershire Sauce, cayenne pepper, and garlic.
    4. Form the mixture into 8 patties.
    5. Grill patties until no more pink inside (about five minutes each side).
