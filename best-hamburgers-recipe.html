Best Hamburger Recipe

The best Hamburger Recipe for grilling or cooking on the stovetop.

These juicy homemade patties are made with zesty herbs and ready to go in under 30 minutes.

\

Ingredients

  • Ground chuck use 80% lean for best results.
  • Crushed crackers or Panko bread crumbs.
  • Worcestershire Sauce.
  • Egg (for binding).
  • Milk.
  • Seasonings: Salt, Garlic Powder, Onion Powder, and Pepper.

Steps

  1. Get a large mixing bowl
  2. Put the ground chuck, crushed crackers, egg, Worcestershire Sauce, milk, and the spices in the bowl.
  3. Mix together with your hands until smooth.
  4. Next press your meat down into an even disk.
  5. Cut into 6 even patties or 12 thinner patties.
  6. Lay out a baking sheet lined with wax paper.
  7. Lay the patties on the wax paper one at a time.
  8. Press patties down to thickness desired, You normally want patties larger then the bun.
  9. Press a dent in the middle of the patty so they don't puff up.
  10. Now you can Preheat the grill or skillet to 350 to 400 degrees.
  11. When the grill or skillet is heated to desired temperature, you may placed the patties to cook.
  12. Cook for a few minutes then flip.
  13. Check patties for doneness.
  14. Optionally you can add cheese while they cook for a melted look and feel.
  15. When done, remove burgers to a place to rest while you get bun ready.
  16. Dress bun as you like and place burger on bun.
  17. Enjoy the food.
