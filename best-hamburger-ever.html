Best Hamburger Ever

The best Hamburger for grilling in the Summertime.

These burgers are jammed packed with all kinds of flavor

Ingredients

  • 1 and a half pounds of ground beef.
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped.
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese or colby jack.
  • 1 ounch dry onion mix.
  • 1 egg.
  • 1 clove garlic, minced.
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder.
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce.
  • 1 teaspoon Worcestershire Sauce.
  • 1 teaspoon dried parsley.
  • 1 teaspoon dried Basil.
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano.
  • half teaspoon crushed dried rosemary.
  • salt and pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to heat.
  2. grab a large mixing bowl.
  3. Mix ground beef,onion, cheese, onion soup mix, egg, garlic, garlic powder, soy sauce, Worcestershire Sauce, parsley, basil, oregano, rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  4. Form the mixture into 4 patties.
  5. Grill patties until no more pink inside (about five minutes each side).
